Taming Yogi's separation anxiety

Hey, I'm Shane! About a year ago, I adopted an 8 year old labradoodle named Yogi from a family that was moving and couldn't take him.

I was told he had some separation anxiety, but I was okay with that since at most, I would only have to leave him by himself a day or two a week.

Unfortunately, he never wanted to be left alone.

This was recorded on my way home one evening. The hole is from a time Yogi locked me out of my apartment and I had to break in. Yikes!

Yogi would bark, howl, and scratch at the front door anytime I was away for more than a few minutes.

In fact, you may have noticed the video was taken through a hole in my front door. I had to break into my own apartment because Yogi locked me out while scratching at the door.

Needless to say, Yogi had severe separation anxiety and since you're here, I'm guessing your dog does to some degree as well.

Here's how to help your pup!

The good news is, there are things you can do to help, but they aren't quick fixes. It took about two months of consistent training before Yogi was okay with being home by himself.

I want to help you and your pup as well, so I put together a list of training resources I used. These resources go over the two types of separation anxiety and give you step-by-step instructions for how to fix each.
